## Let's Go ! 

Tongeren, Belgium's first city, fascinates with its Roman and medieval relics and beautiful basilica. A tough cycle route with a few steep climbs through a backdrop of impressive castles and fortresses. You also pass Herstappe, the smallest municipality of our country.

Pliniuspark

Fonteindreef 8

TONGEREN
